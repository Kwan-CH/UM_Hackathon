About Us

We make surplus food easy to rescue.

FoodRescue is a coordination platform that connects food donors with local NGOs. Our goal is to reduce waste and get safe, edible food to people who need it.

See Benefits Request a Pickup

Our Mission

Cut food waste by making donations simple, fast, and reliable—so communities can get more meals with less friction.

How It Works

  1. Donor submits donation details using the pickup assistant.
  2. We match the request to an available NGO / driver route.
  3. Pickup is confirmed and completed within the agreed window.

What We Accept

Prepared meals, baked goods, and packaged items that are safe to handle and ready for pickup. Clear labeling and packaging helps speed up coordination.

Partner With Us

Whether you donate daily or weekly, we help you build a consistent process. Start by sending one request to see how smooth it can be.
